It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks is a popular coffee house that was founded in 1971 by three people who met while attending the University of San Francisco. These three former students Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker pooled their talents and passion for coffee to start a company that would eventually grow to be worth more than $10 billion by 2014. The first gourmet coffee store opened in Seattle, Washington on March 30th, 1971. In 1987 the original Starbucks owners sold the company to Howard Schultz, and expansion began, growing from only six stores in 1987 to 46 by 1989. The first Starbucks outside North America opened in 1996 in Japan, and since then has expanded to countries all over the world.
